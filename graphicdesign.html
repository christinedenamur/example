#1 sensations

For this work we had to visualize sensations by using two different things/objects/situations. I made it myself a bit easier by beginning to illustrate some Flemish metaphors. Like a cabbage in the throat means in Flemish that you have this sensation in your throat because you’re scared, or can’t talk about something. I didn’t apply this on every poster, just a few. For example, there’s one who gives you the sensation of being under water, which isn’t a metaphor.

#2 what if

I made this work in my first year of Graphic Design. It’s based on a question, “What if…”. The answer to this question had to be represented in a book format. I chose “What if you don’t have an hairdryer to dry your hair?”. The solution to this question was to show how to dry it with the wind. As there are different kinds of winds, your hair will have a different look according to their own speed. I tried to put a chronology in the book. It begins with a gentle breeze and ends with a hurricane.

#3 record sleeve

This is also a work I made in my first year. We had the choice between five albums and I chose the album of Serge Gainsbourg “L’homme à tête de chou”. For this sleeve, I used some fragments of the statue “The Rape of Proserpina” by Gian Lorenzo Bernini because the whole album contains sexual lyrics about Serge Gainsbourg’s ex wife. The hands on this wife’s body make it looks like she’s a lust object. It’s that lust that comes back in the album. I wanted to create a sleeve that could be opened and that had an in- and outside. The inside is a naked woman, but her body is cut to pieces. I did this because in the last song the main character of the album kills the woman. Also the vinyl is coming through the middle of her body to accentuate the cuttings.

#4 newspaper

For this work we had to make a newspaper, about one social problem. Ichose the problem of unemployment caused by automation in the industry. Because this had to be in a newspaper format, I used advertisements as my template for every left page. On the right page and under the advertisements, there is a picture of a robot making a car without any help of a human being. I used also an existing text to complete the subject I chose. In the beginning of this newspaper, you see a lot of advertisements on the left and on the right the picture I was talking about, but i’ts still unclear what it represents. As you go further the number of advertisements decreases, but the robot in the picture becomes more and more visible.

#5 wunderkammer

This is the first work I made in my second year. We had to make a Cabinet of curiosities but in a book-form of minimum 80 pages. We could use some images we liked or inspired us. I began to look for all kind of albino people, but also plants and animals because of their common characteristic; white. But a 80 pages book with only albino’s could look too monotone and boring, so I began to search for a lot of white objects, different white textiles,… Among those objects I had a lot of Mary statues made of white marble. Except the fact that these were white, I also liked the draping and the fact that Mary is dressed from her head to her feet. Finally this became a religious little book. I used Catholic Songbooks and little bibles to find my typography. The red sentences refer to songs about Mary from those songbooks. We also had to use one text chosen by our teachers. This text surrounds the red sentences. I wanted to make different chapters linked to each other. The second chapter refers to the draping of Mary’s clothes but is also a reaction on the hiding aspect of her body. I used pictures of naked bodies but placed them in a way that the body-lines continue from one page to another. The third chapter is a reaction on visualizing the naked body. Sometimes these pictures are censored with pixels. So at first we see pixeled pictures of people or plants but in the end these images are projected on these people. You should see a mix of pixels and the clear face of the subject.

#6 infographic

Poster (33,071 inch x 48,03 inch) that must prove right any conspiracy theory we like. I chose the conspiracy theory of Tupac being alive. Many believe he faked his death to live in peace without any gangsters around him asking him money or getting him in problems. I chose this because I liked Tupac’s tattoos. Getting them all on one page made it become a graphically interesting aspect. Also the FBI and autopsy reports were very interesting for my design. At the end I used a newspaper to prove his death is wrong. This results in a poster with the censored squares of the FBI files, the autopsy drawing and the tattoos all in that newspaper cover upon several autopsy files.

#7 tangerine dream

For this project we had to design 3 vinyl covers of 2 already chosen artists and one of our choice. On top of this design we also had to design a type-font that must be seen on the vinyl covers. 


The design of the covers was made thanks to the letters I created. They have been made with the idea to create a third dimension, a 3D letter. The form of these letters has already a curtain presence. The only thing I did, was to place them for every cover in a different way. The 3D bowl that surrounds the letters shows an evolution from the first to the third vinyl. You can see that the bowl around “Lifestyle” consists of three 3D circles making a bowl. For “Phaedra” there's one of those circles missing to finally end up with only one circle for “Zeit”. This bowl gives the three vinyls a connection with each other, although the music isn't linked with each other. If you look at the back covers, you can see that every block of text attempts to create depth, but each with a different appearance. 


For the inside of the cover we also had to design the inner-sleeve. This design is different for every vinyl as it is made following the subject or the title of the record.

#8 clash of the titans

This title is also known for his movies, but what I had to do here

was to make a website following this theme. 


The titan I had to represent was Zeus. He's considered as the most powerful and elegant God, but with his little secrets which most myths don't reveal. This kind of dark side of Zeus interested me and I tried to link it with today's internet. Although he mostly shows bravery in myths, he's also known for his romances with a lot of women (around 90), or killing innocent people only for his own good. I wanted to go further with the fact he had many lovers. I linked this item with the pornography we can find on every corner of the world wide web, but also all the spam we get. It's there and everybody knows it, but we don't necessarily see it. With this idea, I wanted to create an interactive website that would be very easy to use on an Ipad or tablet, but also on certain laptops. As you can see in the video, once I move from left to right the spam pictures but also the pornographic pictures appear on the screen, which is annoying. Only if you can keep the tablet horizontal, columns will rise up and show you a present, where Zeus is hidden. This part on the website is a representation of Zeus how we know him, I only wanted to exaggerate a little. You complete the mission than you get a present and are able to see him, this shows the power of Zeus and how he can submit us. Once the present open, you get the choice to click on one of the stereo's to receive some heroic songs. 

#9 minima docta

Minima Docta is a platform where everyday subjects, but mostly linked to the art scene, can be discussed and debated. The meetings were organized by teachers of Luca Ghent. 


As graphic designers of Luca Ghent, we were asked to make posters for every meeting that was organized. Since we are 22 third grade bachelor students, I only had to make 2 posters for two meetings. The first one was “Kunst zoekt school” (Art looks for school) and the second one was for the screening of the documentary “London, the modern Babylon.” This last one was made with all different letters coming from an archive I compiled of posters dating from 1920 to 2010 used in London. Following the same idea as the director of this documentary did, using only images coming from the archives of the BBC to make his film, I did the same using the posters of my archive.

#10 drift

Drift is a young new organization in Ghent helping young artists (living in Ghent) to evolve in their career. It mostly supports performance art, theater and dance. As a new organization it needed : an identity, a website, own paper and cards. I wanted to show Drift as an organization that gives an evolution to young artists. This evolution was for me the most important thing. I tried to show this in a very abstract way. I wanted that dots became a line by reducing the space between the dots each time they cross one of the letters of Drift. Upon this idea, this design gives the read direction of the eye as it shows you how to read the name Drift. With this in mind, I wanted to use this power of deciding the reading-directions, in any text that they might use. This is why you'll see dots dispersed along the text, which will give the reader the intention to read the area of the dots, first. 


I used the same way of thinking for their website. Once you load their page, you'll see a list of coming up events or news. Once you click somewhere, a little green ball will follow that area (this as a link to the reading-directions). This site is made following a very basic layout with as purpose to be informative.

#11 vriendenboekje

This book is well known by every child as it consists of filling it up with all kind of questions for your friends. With our class in Luca Ghent, we received every week a random question that we had to answer, each time personally, with a design on (A4). We did this five times to finally gather every work of every student and make a book of it with a self-made cover-design. 


I separated this book in two, all the black and white designs together and all the colored designs together. Which means I had to make two covers but no back cover as I had made two beginnings. I also wanted to make it possible to put another picture than mine on the covers, so that this book could be of anyone of this class. Inside the book I classified all the works by question and sorted them all according to the names of the students.
